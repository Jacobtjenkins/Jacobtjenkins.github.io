Introduction

Trendy Tees

Trendy Tees is a local small business located in downtown San Luis Obispo. They sell t-shirts from popular streetwear brands like Santa Cruz and Vans but you can also order custom prints aswell. Although t-shirts are the main product sold at Trendy Tees, you can also purchase cool stickers in variety packs or by themselves.

Target audience

The target audience for Trendy Tees is teenagers and young adults between the ages of 14 and 24. Most of the t-shirts we sell incorporate bright colors with modern minimalist designs that are in touch with today's streetwear scene.

The primary goal of Trendy Tees is to provide customers with high quality, stylish t-shirts at affordable prices. Each week the company has a new special offer that gives customers a chance to save some money while shopping for t-shirts that fit their personal styles.
