About Us

Ice Cream is in our blood. This family owned business has been around since 1942, with with recipes that have been passed down from generation to generation. All ice cream is made in house and taste tested daily, ensuring that it is up to our standards as well as yours. Our company values family and happiness above all so we hope to put a smile on your faces from the moment you first take a bite of our chilly, delicous treats.

One of the best things about ice cream is that, while almost everyone has a favorite flavor, most people don’t have the SAME favorite flavor. Every day our ice cream inventors wake up with one goal – to create someone’s next favorite flavor. Sure, sometimes that thinking leads to some wacky stuff (hello “turkey dinner ice cream”, we’re talking about you!), but other times it yields Blueberries ‘n Cream or Mocha Almond Fudge. And those are the days we live for.
